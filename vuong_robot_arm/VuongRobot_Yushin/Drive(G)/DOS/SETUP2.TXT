
Welcome to the E Editor.

Changes have been made by SETUP and saved to your AUTOEXEC.BAT and
CONFIG.SYS files.

Press the CTRL+N key to edit or view each file.  Then:

   Press F3 to close the file without saving any additional
    changes, or

   Press F4 to save any additional changes you have made to
    the file.

To get additional help, press the F1 key.
