Ejector Interlock (Interlock 1/Interlock 2/No)
Åú Interlock 1
After the end-of-arm tool arrives at the take-out position, it waits the amount of time set by the timer. Then, the ejector command is inputted.

Åú Interlock 2
After the end-of-arm tool arrives at the descent position, it waits the amount of time set by the timer. Then, the ejector command is inputted.

Åú No
The timing of the ejector pin motion is controlled by the injection molding machine.
